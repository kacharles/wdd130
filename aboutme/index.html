"Northern Cyprus is a self-declared state located on the northern part of the island of Cyprus in the eastern mediterranean. It was established in 1983 following a Turkish melitary intervention in response to a coup by Greek Cypriots seeking to unify the island with Greece. Northern Cyprus is recognized only by Turkey, and is considered by the international community to be occupied territory of the Republic of Cyprus. The economy of Northern Cyprus is heavily dependent on Turkey, with tourism and education being the primary sources of income." -wiki
